In the silence within the church, only the whistling of the wind and the sound of falling leaves on the walls remained. The once bustling shelter has become empty, like a forgotten ruin, witnessing the passage of years through the storm. However, despite the departure of the people, the church still stands there, a lonely watch over this barren land. The desolate town is gradually swallowed by time, everything seems to return to the original quiet, only the church quietly stood there, silently witnessing all the changes.
